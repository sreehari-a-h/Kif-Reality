Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common Dubai real estate investment questions

Can foreigners buy property in Dubai?

Yes, foreigners can buy freehold properties in designated areas of Dubai, including Downtown Dubai, Dubai Marina, Palm Jumeirah, and many other prime locations. KIF Reality specializes in guiding international investors through the entire purchase process.

What is the average ROI for Dubai properties?

Dubai properties typically offer 5-8% rental yields, with some prime locations offering higher returns. Our ROI analysis team provides detailed calculations based on current market data, property type, and location to help you make informed investment decisions.

What are off-plan properties?

Off-plan properties are units sold before construction completion, often with flexible payment plans during construction. These typically offer lower prices and high appreciation potential. KIF Reality works with top developers to offer the best off-plan opportunities.

How long does the property buying process take?

For ready properties, the process typically takes 2-4 weeks from offer acceptance to handover. Off-plan properties follow construction timelines. Our team manages all documentation, approvals, and legal processes to ensure smooth transactions.

Do you provide property management services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive property management services including tenant sourcing, rent collection, maintenance coordination, and investment portfolio management. This ensures hassle-free ownership and optimized returns.
